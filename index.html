About Me

I am a software developer with a unique background in agriculture, I bring a fresh perspective and a strong work ethic to the world of technology. My journey began as a farmer, where I cultivated a deep appreciation for hard work and the natural world's harmony. Intrigued by the limitless possibilities at the nexus of farming and technology, I transitioned into software engineering. Through dedicated study and hands-on experience, I've acquired proficiency in a range of technologies essential to modern development. I've completed courses in software engineering, honing my skills in JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB. My tech stack reflects my commitment to leveraging cutting-edge tools to build innovative solutions. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for accessibility, I strive to create user-friendly experiences that empower and enrich.

Skill Set

responsive design

With careful attention to detail, I specialize in creating websites that effortlessly adjust to various devices and screen sizes. By skillfully designing flexible layouts, fluid grids, and using CSS media queries, I guarantee a seamless viewing experience for users across different platforms.

Creative projects
